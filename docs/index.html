How the forecast works +

Each couple's TikTok posts and comments are scored on four signals, then combined:

HypeScore = 100 × (w₁·ShareOfVoice + w₂·Sentiment + w₃·Velocity + w₄·Reach), where each signal is a z-score across the current couples and the weights are the sliders above.

Share of Voice: how much of the total chatter is about this couple. Sentiment: engagement-weighted positivity of captions and comments (VADER), refined by a Claude pass that reads voting intent (save vs. eliminate) and catches sarcasm. Velocity: whether buzz is heating up or cooling within the scan window. Reach: distinct accounts talking, capped at one per author so a single superfan can't inflate the count.

A couple that is loud but loathed (lots of chatter, but the room wants them gone) is flagged hate-watch and pushed below the safe line, because volume alone fools naive models.
